# Contributing to Awesome AbacatePay

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the **Awesome AbacatePay** list!  
This repository is a **curated list** of high-quality resources related to the AbacatePay ecosystem.

Contributions are welcome, but please follow the guidelines below to keep the list useful and consistent.

---

## What Can Be Added

You may suggest:

- Libraries, SDKs, or tools that integrate with AbacatePay
- Official or community-driven projects
- Tutorials, guides, or high-quality blog posts
- Open source projects actively maintained

All submissions **must be relevant to AbacatePay**.

---

## What Will NOT Be Accepted

- Low-effort or spammy links
- Pure self-promotion without clear value
- Broken, outdated, or abandoned projects
- Affiliate links or monetized referral links
- Duplicate resources

Maintainers reserve the right to reject submissions that do not meet quality standards.

---

## How to Contribute

1. Fork this repository.
2. Create a branch for your change.
3. Add your resource following the existing format.
4. Open a Pull Request with a **clear description** of why the resource is valuable.

> For simple fixes (typos, broken links), opening a PR directly is fine.

---

## Formatting Rules

- Keep descriptions concise and objective.
- Use consistent markdown formatting.
- One resource per list item.

## Review Process

All Pull Requests are reviewed by maintainers.
Feedback or requested changes are part of the process.

Thank you for helping keep the AbacatePay ecosystem organized and high-quality.
